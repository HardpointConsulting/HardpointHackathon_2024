Titlr.

Titlr is a state-of-the-art application engineered to automate the extraction of vital information from scientific documents and generate alternative titles based on their content. Ideal for researchers, academics, and students alike, Titlr empowers users to swiftly analyze documents, pinpoint essential details such as titles and author names, and propose alternative titles that encapsulate the document's core concepts.
